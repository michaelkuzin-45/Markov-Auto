Who We Are

Markov Automotive.
RaceSpec Exotics.

We're a Springfield, Virginia–based premium vehicle rental company operating as Markov Automotive, under the RaceSpec Exotics brand. Our fleet represents some of the finest production automobiles available today — curated for clients who expect more than a transportation solution.

We handle every reservation directly. No platforms. No third-party fees. No surprises. When you contact us, you speak to the people who own and maintain the cars — and who care about your experience as much as the vehicles themselves.

How We Operate

Our standards.

01

Direct Relationships

Every reservation is handled personally. You're not navigating a platform — you're speaking with the people who own these cars. Questions get answered quickly, terms are discussed honestly, and we work with you to make the experience right.

02

Maintained to Standard

Our vehicles are professionally serviced, cleaned, and inspected before every rental. What you see in the listing photos is exactly what you pick up. We take pride in presentation — it reflects how we feel about the vehicles and the people driving them.

03

No Hidden Costs

Daily rates and deposit requirements are stated upfront. We don't add fees at the last step or obscure terms in fine print. Pricing is transparent from the first conversation to the final confirmation.

04

Same-Day Response

When you submit a reservation request, we respond the same day. We understand that timing matters — whether you're planning a special occasion or need a vehicle on short notice, we do our best to accommodate.

The Collection

Eight vehicles.
